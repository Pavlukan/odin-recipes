Chicken Kiev from Eastern Europe

Description

Chicken Kiev or Chicken Kyiv is a dish made of chicken, pounded and rolled around cold butter, then coated with egg and breadcrumbs.

Though it has disputed origins, the dish is particularly popular in the post-Soviet states? as well as in several other countries of the former Eastern Bloc.

Ingredients

  • 120 grammes of skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/3 cup of butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 2 eggs
  • 3 tablespoons of water
  • 1/4 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1/4 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of dill weed
  • 1/2 cup of all-purpose flour
  • 1/2 cup of dry bread crumbs
  • 2 cups of vegetable oil
  • 1/2 of lemon, sliced
  • 1/4 cup of chopped fresh parsley

Steps

  1. Place each chicken breast between a sheet of wax paper and pound, using a mallet or rolling pin, to about 3 mm.
  2. Wrap and refrigerate the chicken breasts
  3. In a small bowl combine the softened butter, 1/2 teaspoon of pepper and 1 teaspoon of garlic powder. Mix it well.
  4. Spread the mixture into a 5x10 cm rectangle on a piece of aluminum foil; freeze until firm.
  5. When butter is firm, cut into 8 equal size pieces and place one piece on each chicken breast. Fold in edges of chicken and roll to encase butter completely. Secure with toothpicks.
  6. In a small bowl, beat eggs with water.
  7. In a shallow dish or bowl, mix together the 1/4 teaspoon pepper, 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder, dill weed and flour.
  8. Place bread crumbs in a separate shallow dish or bowl.
  9. Dip chicken rolls in seasoned flour, then egg mixture, then crumbs.
  10. Place coated chicken in a shallow dish, cover and refrigerate for about 30 minutes.
  11. Heat oil in a large skillet over medium high heat.
  12. When oil is hot, deep fry chicken rolls for about 5 minutes, then turn over and deep fry for another 5 minutes until golden and cooked through, with no pink showing inside.
  13. Drain on paper towels, garnish with lemon slices and parsley and serve.
