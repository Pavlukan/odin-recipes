Draniki from Eastern Europe

Description

Draniki is one of the most popular and famous dishes of the Belarusian cuisine.

Although there are many similar recipes for potato pancakes in various countries, Belarusian draniki is famous for its rich taste, national culinary secrets and the floury Belarusian potatoes – bulba – with their fluffy, dry texture that is perfect for making potato pancakes.

Not to mention that they are quite easy to make.

Ingredients

  • 12 potatoes.
  • 1 onion
  • Oil
  • Salt

Steps

  1. Peel, wash and grate potatoes and the onion. Use a grater with small holes.
  2. Add salt and mix until a smooth even consistency is obtained.
  3. Heat the frying pan with vegetable oil.
  4. Put a full tablespoon of mixture from the bowl onto the frying pan and form a small pancake.
  5. Flip over once browned and crispy (about 2-3 minutes each side).
  6. Serve hot with sour cream. Enjoy!
