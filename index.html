Recipes from Eastern Europe

This website will provide you with the most saliva-inducing recipes from Eastern Europe.

The recipes are primary from countries such as Russia, Belarus, Ukraine, Hungary, and Poland.

Here's the list of all the recipes:

  • Draniki (Potato Pancakes)
  • Borscht (Eastern European soup)
  • Goulash (Hungarian traditional dish)
  • Chicken-Kiev (Dish straight from the Russian Empire)
  • Kvass (Low alcoholic Slavic beverage)
  • Babka (Sweet braided bread from Jewish communities of Eastern Europe)
